Block 6 · Historical Interpretation

At ~126 months, mRNA Cancer Vaccines show one of the longest diffusion lags in the dataset — consistent with technologies requiring extended clinical validation before public attention consolidates. The L value reflects the distance between first therapeutic trials (January 2015) and sustained public attention (August 2025).

The political amplification component (RFK Jr., anti-vaccine discourse) is historically atypical. Most STRUCTURAL technologies in the dataset were triggered by product launches, regulatory acts, or scientific milestones. Political controversy as an attention trigger introduces upward bias in peak scores and may reduce structural durability.
