How to Read This Dataset

Layer Classification

Layer A — Hard Infrastructure
L-Comparable Cases
Phenomena with documented operational emergence and institutional legibility trajectory. L values are cross-comparable within this layer. Eligible for sector averaging and benchmark calculations. t_start defined as operational launch or regulatory first recognition.
Layer B — Attention Phenomena
Regime-Classifiable Only
Phenomena driven by speculation, cultural moments, or exogenous shocks. L values are not cross-comparable with Layer A. Eligible for regime classification and pattern analysis. Included to document the full spectrum of diffusion dynamics — not excluded because they are less valid.

4-Regime Taxonomy

Regime 1
Policy-Trigger
Slow ramp → regulatory breakout → sustained consolidation

PQC, Stablecoins, CBDC, ESG, EU AI Act
Regime 2
Institutional Mass
Multi-year invisibility → explosive single-month breakout

RWA, Tokenized Deposits, EV Infra, Quantum Computing
Regime 3
Market-Narrative
Speculation cycles, media amplification, price events

Bitcoin, Crypto, DeFi, Remote Work, GenAI
Regime 4
Shock / Spoke
Single-event spike → memory-zero collapse

GDPR, NFT partial, Clubhouse

Stationarity Flag

STABLE
Organic diffusion. No exogenous shock. L fully comparable cross-cluster. Eligible for sector averaging.
SEMI-STABLE
Partial external influence. L usable with declared caution. Flagged in cross-cluster comparisons.
NON-STATIONARY
Exogenous shock dominant. L excluded from averaging. Included in regime classification only.
